# Outreach Email Template

Use this as a base. Keep drafts short, truthful, and specific.

## Subject Options

```text
{{role_title}} at {{company}}
```

```text
Interest in {{company}}'s {{role_title}} role
```

```text
{{role_title}} candidate - {{candidate_name}}
```

## Body

```text
Hi {{contact_first_name}},

I saw {{company}} is hiring for {{role_title}} and wanted to reach out directly.

I am especially interested because {{specific_company_or_role_reason}}.

My background lines up with the role through {{truthful_relevant_proof_point_1}} and {{truthful_relevant_proof_point_2}}. I have attached my resume for context.

Would you be open to considering me for the role, or pointing me to the right person on the team?

Best,
{{candidate_name}}
{{candidate_email}}
{{candidate_phone_optional}}
{{linkedin_or_portfolio_optional}}
```

## Rules

- Do not pretend to know the contact.
- Do not claim referral status.
- Do not use flattery that is not specific.
- Do not mention unavailable facts.
- Do not include more than two proof points unless the user asks for a longer note.
- Do not send. Create a draft only.
