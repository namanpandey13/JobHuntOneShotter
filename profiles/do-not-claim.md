# Do Not Claim

This file is a hard constraint for resume tailoring, outreach, and applications.

The agent must not claim anything here unless the user later adds explicit evidence to the profile, resume, or approved notes.

## Credentials Not To Claim

- Do not claim degrees, certificates, licenses, or coursework that are not listed in the profile.
- Do not claim security clearance unless explicitly provided.
- Do not claim patents, publications, awards, or open-source maintainer status unless explicitly provided.

## Experience Not To Claim

- Do not claim employers or clients not listed in the profile or resume.
- Do not claim job titles not listed in the profile or resume.
- Do not claim people management, budget ownership, executive ownership, or hiring authority unless explicitly provided.
- Do not claim production ownership, on-call responsibility, compliance responsibility, or security responsibility unless explicitly provided.

## Skills Not To Claim

- Do not claim programming languages, frameworks, cloud platforms, databases, or tools that are not listed in the profile, resume, or approved notes.
- Do not imply expert-level ability unless the profile provides evidence.
- Do not turn exposure into production experience.

## Metrics Not To Claim

- Do not invent numbers.
- Do not round up metrics.
- Do not add revenue, cost savings, latency, reliability, headcount, user count, or scale metrics unless provided.

## Legal Or Sensitive Information Not To Claim

- Do not claim work authorization status unless provided in the sensitive answers file or confirmed by the user.
- Do not answer sponsorship questions unless provided in the sensitive answers file or confirmed by the user.
- Do not answer EEO, veteran, disability, criminal history, background check, consent, or legal acknowledgement questions unless provided in the sensitive answers file or confirmed by the user.
